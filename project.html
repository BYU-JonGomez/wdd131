This project is about turistic places and events in Viña del mar, Chile.

In this 3 pages project, you will find information about the most important turistic places and events in Viña del Mar, Chile, incluiding some statistics related to the number of visitors, the most visited places, and the most popular activities.

Page 1 will describe the city, and will show specific information about its population, weather, its location in the map and other relevant information related, will include pictures and data, to sort information will create some tables to show statistics, beside will include relevant information for emergency contacts, like police, fireman, lifeguard, and ambulance.

Page 2 will show the most visited places in the city, including the most popular beaches, parks, and other turistic places. In this case will include images, text and tables.

Page 3 will show the most popular activities in the city, including the most popular sports, cultural activities, and other activities that the visitors can do in the city. In this case will include images, text and tables.

The project has for objective show this city to the world, and to promote the turism in the best city for livign in Chile. The project will include images, and other multimedia elements to make the information more attractive and interesting for the visitors.
