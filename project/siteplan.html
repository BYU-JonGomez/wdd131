Viña del Mar - Site Plan

Site Name - KnowingViñadelMar (KVM)

This name represents a platform dedicated to showcasting the beautiful coastal city of Viña del Mar in Chile, providing detailed desscriptions, information about tourist attractions, and permanent events.

Site Porpuse

The website aims to be a comprehensive guide for visitors and locals interested in exploring Viña del Mar. It will provide detailed descriptions of the city, information on must-visit tourist spots, and a calendar of events happening in the area.

Scenarios

  • Scenario 1: A family is planning a vacation to Viña del Mar and wants to know the best places to visit.
  • Scenario 2: A group of friends is looking for fun activities to do in Viña del Mar over the weekend.
  • Scenario 3: A group of friends is looking for places and activities to define their next vacation destination. Can this city offer what they're looking for?.

Color Schema

  • Primary Color: #1E90FF (Dodger Blue) - Used for headings, buttons, and accents to convey a vibrant and welcoming vibe.
  • Secondary Color: #F0F8FF (Alice Blue) - Used for the background and body text to ensure readability and a clean design.

Typography

  • Font 1: Montserrat - Used for headings to provide a modern and clean look.
  • Font 2: Lora - Used for body text to ensure readability and a friendly appearance.

Wireframe

  • Header: Logo and navigation menu, and city name
  • Main part 1: will include a picture and general information about Viña del Mar, like foundation date, inhabitants, location on the map, and miscellaneous information.
  • Main part 2: will include a picture and specific information related to features and landmarks. Also will include specific information about how Viña del Mar is recognized in the world.
  • Main part 3: will include a map, where people could know where this city is located in Chile.
  • Main part 4: will include a table with emergency contacts, like police, fireman, lifeguard, and ambulance.
